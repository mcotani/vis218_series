Making Our Own

When I returned home after some time in college, I spent some time experimenting with my childhoo yukata, which was bought at UniQlo (so no growing seams here :( ) and was way too small for me. Cutting open the flowing undersleeve part yielded an interesting, rectangular shape in the shoulders and I decided to experiment with a separate piece of thicker cloth (I originally used hemp twill) to see if the piece could work as a long jacket.
